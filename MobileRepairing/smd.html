English

  1. SMD resistors two types one repairable two non repairable
  2. They are called fusible resistances as they are used instead of fuses in cellphone PCBs
  3. Jumper wire can be applied directly when these resistors are open
  4. These resistors have a certain value so when the resistors are open they should be replaced with a resistor of the same value
  5. Do not put a jumper wire in place of this resistance
