English

  1. A coil of wire made of an inductor is called an inductor or a coil and is denoted by the letter 'L'
  2. When a voltage is applied to a coil, a magnetic field is created in it.
  3. The formation of a magnetic field in the coil blocks the carrier frequencies from the coil
  4. Depending on the supply we give to the coil, the magnetic field generated in it also varies
  5. A coil is connected in series from supply to load
  6. The coil should not be connected parallel to the supply
  7. The coil only opens and is replied either with another coil or with a jumper wire as the case may be
