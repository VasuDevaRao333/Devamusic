English

  1. In the above circuit the transformer takes the AC voltage and lowers the AC voltage in the secondary
  2. The diode converts the AC voltage in the secondary of the transformer into DC and forwards it
  3. Diode converts AC to DC but not pure DC
  4. The diode then converts the pulsating DC to pure DC by the polarity capacitor and is sent to the load so the load operates continuously.
  5. This is called eliminator and charger is used as eliminator
